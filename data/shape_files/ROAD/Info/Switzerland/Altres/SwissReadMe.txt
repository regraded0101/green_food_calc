E Road Census 2005
Swiss data

The directory "MapDetails" contains several files that might be useful for
establishing the European publication. These files are exported by ARCMAP (ESRI), Version 8.3.

Information on the counting posts with situation of the counting posts according to the
Swiss coordinate system:
CountingPosts.dbf
CountingPosts.prj
CountingPosts.shp
CountingPosts.shx

Information on the road sections: section number, section geometry and attributes of the section: 
ERoads.dbf
ERoads.prj
ERoads.shp
ERoads.shx
